Texas CDL Practice Test — FAQ

Quick answers about the Texas CDL knowledge tests and how to study with PassRoute.

▶ Start practicing free

Is PassRoute really free?

Yes — all Texas CDL practice tests are 100% free, with no account or payment. There are 2,200+ questions with answers and explanations.

What score do I need to pass?

At least 80% on each Texas CDL knowledge test. PassRoute uses the same 80% passing standard.

What languages are available?

English, Spanish, Russian, Turkish, and Hindi — questions, options, and explanations.

Which tests do I need for a Class A CDL?

Four required: Texas Special Requirements, General Knowledge, Air Brakes, and Combination Vehicles. Endorsements (HazMat, Tanker, Doubles/Triples, Passenger, School Bus) are optional.

How many questions are on the real General Knowledge test?

50 questions; you need 80% to pass. PassRoute's General Knowledge pool has 500+ questions for variety.

Is PassRoute affiliated with the Texas DPS?

No. It is an independent study aid based on the official Texas CDL Handbook, not affiliated with the DPS, DMV, or any government agency.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes — any mobile or desktop browser, and you can add it to your home screen. Progress saves on your device.
