How to get your driver license in Wyoming

  1. Study the Wyoming Driver Handbook and pass the knowledge test.
  2. Pass the vision test and gather your required documents.
  3. Apply with the Wyoming WYDOT and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your learner permit for the required time, then take the road test.
