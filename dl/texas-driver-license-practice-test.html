How to get your driver license in Texas

  1. Study the Texas Driver Handbook and pass the knowledge test.
  2. Pass the vision test and gather your required documents.
  3. Apply with the Texas DPS and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your learner permit for the required time, then take the road test.
