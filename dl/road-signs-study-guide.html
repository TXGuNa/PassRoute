Sample questions

What shape is a STOP sign?

  1. Octagon (8 sides) ✓ Correct answer
  2. Diamond
  3. Circle
  4. Vertical rectangle

Why: A STOP sign is the only octagonal (8-sided) sign and requires a full stop.

What does an upside-down (inverted) triangle sign mean?

  1. Stop
  2. Yield ✓ Correct answer
  3. No passing
  4. School zone

Why: An inverted triangle means YIELD, so give the right of way and slow or stop if needed.

What does a pennant sign (a horizontal triangle on the left side of the road) mean?

  1. School zone ahead
  2. Stop completely
  3. No Passing Zone ✓ Correct answer
  4. Railroad crossing

Why: A pennant, a horizontal triangle placed on the left side of the road, marks a No Passing Zone.

What does a diamond-shaped sign tell you?

  1. You must come to a full stop
  2. You are entering a one-way street
  3. You have reached the speed limit
  4. There is a hazard or condition ahead ✓ Correct answer

Why: A diamond-shaped sign is a warning sign that indicates a hazard or condition ahead.

What does a pentagon-shaped sign (5 sides, point up) mark?

  1. A school zone or school crossing ✓ Correct answer
  2. A railroad crossing
  3. A no passing zone
  4. A stop intersection

Why: A pentagon (5 sides, point up) marks a school zone or school crossing.

What does a round (circular) road sign warn you about?

  1. A school zone ahead
  2. A railroad crossing ahead ✓ Correct answer
  3. A no-passing zone
  4. A construction zone

Why: A round (circular) sign is an advance warning that a railroad crossing is ahead.
