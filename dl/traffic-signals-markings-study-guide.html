Sample questions

What does a steady green traffic light mean?

  1. Go if the intersection is clear ✓ Correct answer
  2. Stop completely behind the line
  3. Slow down and stop if safe
  4. Treat it as a stop sign

Why: A steady green light means you may go if the intersection is clear, proceeding straight or turning after yielding.

What must you do before turning at a steady green light?

  1. Speed up to clear the turn
  2. Yield to other traffic and pedestrians ✓ Correct answer
  3. Always come to a full stop first
  4. Wait for a green arrow to appear

Why: At a steady green light you may turn only after yielding to other traffic and pedestrians.

What does a steady yellow traffic light mean?

  1. Speed up to clear the intersection
  2. The light just turned green
  3. The light is about to turn red ✓ Correct answer
  4. You have the right of way

Why: A steady yellow light warns that the signal is about to turn red, so stop if you can do so safely.

What should you do at a steady yellow traffic light?

  1. Speed up to beat the red
  2. Always come to a full stop
  3. Ignore it and keep driving
  4. Stop if you can do so safely ✓ Correct answer

Why: A steady yellow light means the light is about to turn red, so stop if you can do so safely and do not speed up.

What does a steady red light mean?

  1. Stop completely until it turns green ✓ Correct answer
  2. Slow down and proceed with caution
  3. Speed up to clear the intersection
  4. Go if the intersection is clear

Why: A steady red light means stop completely behind the line and stay stopped until the light turns green.

At a red light where a right turn is allowed, what must you do first?

  1. Speed up and turn right
  2. Stop fully, then turn if clear ✓ Correct answer
  3. Turn without stopping
  4. Wait for a green arrow

Why: At a red light where right turns are permitted, you must stop completely, then turn right if it is clear and not prohibited by a sign.
