How to get your driver license in New Mexico

  1. Study the New Mexico Driver Handbook and pass the knowledge test.
  2. Pass the vision test and gather your required documents.
  3. Apply with the New Mexico MVD and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your learner permit for the required time, then take the road test.
