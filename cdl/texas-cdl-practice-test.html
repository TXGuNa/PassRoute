How to get your CLP in Texas

  1. Study the Texas CDL Handbook and pass the knowledge tests (80% to pass).
  2. Pass the DOT medical exam and gather your documents.
  3. Apply with the Texas DPS and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your CLP for the required time, then take the road-skills test.
