How to get your CLP in Montana

  1. Study the Montana CDL Handbook and pass the knowledge tests (80% to pass).
  2. Pass the DOT medical exam and gather your documents.
  3. Apply with the Montana MVD and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your CLP for the required time, then take the road-skills test.
