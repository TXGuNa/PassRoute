Sample questions

Which endorsement is required to haul placardable HazMat?

  1. H (or X) ✓ Correct answer
  2. N (or X)
  3. T endorsement
  4. P endorsement

Why: Hauling placardable HazMat requires the H endorsement, or X, which combines the tank and HazMat endorsements.

What does the H/X endorsement require before it can be issued?

  1. A vision and hearing test
  2. A TSA background check and fingerprinting ✓ Correct answer
  3. A medical waiver from the carrier
  4. An air brake skills test

Why: The H/X endorsement requires a TSA security threat assessment, background check, and fingerprinting.

Who classifies, packages, labels, and prepares the shipping papers?

  1. The driver
  2. The carrier
  3. The shipper ✓ Correct answer
  4. The receiver

Why: The shipper classifies, packages, labels, and prepares the shipping papers for a HazMat load.

Who transports the HazMat and reports incidents?

  1. The shipper
  2. The driver
  3. The inspector
  4. The carrier ✓ Correct answer

Why: The carrier transports the HazMat and reports incidents.

What must the driver do with a leaking HazMat shipment?

  1. Refuse it ✓ Correct answer
  2. Label it
  3. Classify it
  4. Package it

Why: The driver must refuse a leaking or improperly marked HazMat shipment and not transport it.

Who is responsible for the safe transport of hazardous materials?

  1. Only the driver
  2. Shipper, carrier, and driver ✓ Correct answer
  3. Only the shipper
  4. Only the carrier

Why: The shipper, carrier, and driver all share responsibility for safely transporting hazardous materials.
