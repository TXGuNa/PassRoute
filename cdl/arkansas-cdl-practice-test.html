How to get your CLP in Arkansas

  1. Study the Arkansas CDL Handbook and pass the knowledge tests (80% to pass).
  2. Pass the DOT medical exam and gather your documents.
  3. Apply with the Arkansas DFA and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your CLP for the required time, then take the road-skills test.
