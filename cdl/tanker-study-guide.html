Sample questions

Why do tank vehicles roll over easily?

  1. High center of gravity ✓ Correct answer
  2. Low center of gravity
  3. Wide wheelbase
  4. Heavy rear axle

Why: Liquid cargo sits high, giving tank vehicles a high center of gravity, so they roll over easily.

How fast should you take curves, ramps, and turns in a tank vehicle?

  1. At the posted speed
  2. Well below the posted speed ✓ Correct answer
  3. Above the posted speed
  4. At the maximum legal speed

Why: Because tank vehicles have a high center of gravity, take curves, ramps, and turns well below the posted speed.

What causes most tank rollovers?

  1. Hard braking
  2. Low tire pressure
  3. Going too fast ✓ Correct answer
  4. Sudden lane changes

Why: Most tank rollovers happen because the driver goes too fast for a turn or ramp.

What helps prevent tank rollovers?

  1. Sudden steering
  2. Hard braking
  3. Shorter following distance
  4. Smooth steering and braking ✓ Correct answer

Why: Smooth steering and braking plus extra following distance help prevent tank rollovers.

In a partly filled tank, what does the liquid do?

  1. Surges back and forth ✓ Correct answer
  2. Stays perfectly still
  3. Sinks to the bottom
  4. Freezes in place

Why: In a partly filled tank, the liquid surges back and forth and side to side.

After you stop, what can forward liquid surge do?

  1. Cause a rollover
  2. Shove the truck forward ✓ Correct answer
  3. Stop the engine
  4. Lock the brakes

Why: After you stop, forward surge can shove the truck forward, such as out into an intersection.
