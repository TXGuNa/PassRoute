How to get your CLP in North Carolina

  1. Study the North Carolina CDL Handbook and pass the knowledge tests (80% to pass).
  2. Pass the DOT medical exam and gather your documents.
  3. Apply with the North Carolina DMV and pay the fees.
  4. Hold your CLP for the required time, then take the road-skills test.
