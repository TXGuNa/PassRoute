Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PassRoute ("we", "us") provides free CDL practice tests at passroute.ai. This policy explains what information is involved when you use the site.

What we collect

PassRoute has no accounts and no login. We do not ask for your name, email, or personal details. The app stores a few settings on your own device using your browser's local storage: your chosen language, light/dark theme, and your practice-test progress, scores, and history. This data stays in your browser and is not sent to us.

Cookies & local storage

We use browser local storage (described above) for the app to function. We do not use our own tracking cookies. Third-party services we use may set their own cookies (see below).

Advertising (Google AdSense)

PassRoute may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • You can control personalized advertising in your Google Ad Settings.
  • Learn how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • Opt out of personalized ads from many vendors at aboutads.info and youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

Where required (e.g., for users in the EEA/UK), we request consent before serving personalized ads.

Hosting

The site is served as static files via Cloudflare, which may process basic request data (such as IP address) for security and delivery, per Cloudflare's privacy practices.

Children

PassRoute is a study tool for commercial-driver-license candidates and is not directed to children under 13.

Your choices

You can clear your stored settings and progress anytime by resetting them in the app or clearing your browser's site data. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
